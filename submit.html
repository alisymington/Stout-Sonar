Submit a pint

Found a class pint of stout? Send it in and we will add it to the radar.

  1. Post your photo on Instagram and tag @stout.radar and use #stoutradar.
  2. Or email it with the bar name and location to hello@stoutradar.com.

You can also add entries by editing /data/reviews.json if you are hosting the site yourself.
